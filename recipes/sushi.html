Salmon Avocado Roll

Description

This is a tried and true classic maki, or roll. Crispy, savory nori wraps fluffy, slightly pungent vinegared rice, with fresh slices of salmon and avocado tucked inside.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Salmon
  • Fresh Avocado
  • Sushi Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Nori Sheets

Steps

  1. Prepare rice by boiling, adding vinegar and sugar
  2. Lay out a sheet of nori
  3. Add and flatten a thin layer of rice to the top side of the nori, covering half of the sheet
  4. Place salmon chunks in a line along the length of the sheet
  5. Place avocado chunks alongside the salmon in another thin line
  6. Roll that badboy up, sealing the nori with tap water
