Soft Egg with Soy Sauce over Rice

Description

A simple, savory comfort dish consisting of a softly cooked egg, drizzled with soy sauce and chopped green onions, on a bed of hot white rice.

Ingredients

  • One Egg
  • Precooked/leftover rice
  • Soy sauce (Lee Kum Kee seasoning soy preferred)
  • Butter, optional
  • Scallions, optional

Steps

  1. Take desired serving of rice and heat in microwave. Optional: lightly fry in pan with butter
  2. In a pan, heat oil and fry egg till underside of whites are solidified (1 min)
  3. Flip egg over to seal other side, making sure yolk is heated sufficiently and not cold inside
  4. Lay egg on top of rice, and drizzle with favorite soy sauce (LKK seasoning soy for extra umami)
  5. Optional: Sprinkle with chopped green onion
  6. Serve and eat hot!
